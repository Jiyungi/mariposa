# Female Fertility Hormone Reference

Real reference ranges used in a basic female fertility workup. Use these to validate "her"
lab fields and to interpret the seed couple's (fictional) labs in the doctor summary.

## Ovarian reserve & baseline hormones

### AMH (Anti-Müllerian Hormone) — ovarian reserve marker
Can be drawn any day of the cycle. Declines with age.

| AMH level | General interpretation |
| --- | --- |
| > 3.0 ng/mL | High (can suggest PCOS) |
| 1.0 – 3.0 ng/mL | Normal / expected reproductive range |
| 0.5 – 1.0 ng/mL | Low–normal / diminishing |
| < 0.5 ng/mL | Low ovarian reserve |

Source: [MedlinePlus AMH test](https://medlineplus.gov/lab-tests/anti-mullerian-hormone-test/).
Content rephrased for compliance.

### Day-3 FSH & Estradiol — drawn on cycle day 2–3
FSH must be measured on day 2 or 3 because levels shift across the cycle.

| Test | Day-3 reference (typical) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| FSH | < 10 mIU/mL favorable; 10–15 borderline; > 15 reduced reserve | |
| Estradiol (E2) | ~ 25–75 pg/mL on day 3 | high day-3 E2 can mask a high FSH |

Sources: [Day-3 FSH & Estradiol guide](https://www.ivfcenterhawaii.com/fertility-treatments-clinic/fsh-and-estradiol-interpreting-day-3-hormone-results/),
[Center for Human Reproduction — interpreting levels](https://centerforhumanreprod.com/interpreting-your-levels).
Content rephrased for compliance.

## Cycle-phase hormones (for context)

| Hormone | Role | Pattern |
| --- | --- | --- |
| LH | Triggers ovulation | Surges ~24–36h before ovulation (what LH strips detect) |
| Progesterone | Confirms ovulation | ~1 week after ovulation should rise; ≈10 ng/mL suggests ovulation occurred |
| TSH / Prolactin | Thyroid / pituitary screen | Often checked because they affect ovulation |

Source: [Wake Forest hormone evaluations](https://www.wakehealth.edu/diagnostic-tools/hormone-evaluations),
[reference values across cycle phases (PubMed)](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/16776638/).
Content rephrased for compliance.
